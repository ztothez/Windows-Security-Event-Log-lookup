Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions published by the developer.

The extensions do not collect, store, track, or share any personal or identifiable user data.

All functionality is initiated explicitly by the user. When a user performs a lookup (e.g. IP address, URL, file hash, or similar technical identifier), the selected value may be sent directly to a relevant third-party public service (such as security or reputation databases) solely to provide the requested result.

The extensions do not retain this data, do not log user activity, and do not use analytics, tracking technologies, or persistent identifiers.

No data is transmitted to the developer or to any servers controlled by the developer.
